Your Solar Journey

Get your solar installed in 5 simple, hassle-free steps.

1

Site Inspection & Design

Our expert engineers visit your site to assess roof space, shading, and energy needs. We then use AI tools to design a custom 3D solar layout optimized for maximum generation.

2

Subsidy & Approvals

We handle all the heavy lifting! Our team submits your application to the PM Surya Ghar National Portal and manages all government documentation to secure your subsidy effortlessly.

3

Expert Installation

Once approved, our certified technicians deliver and install your Tier-1 solar panels, inverters, and mounting structures with precision, ensuring a safe and weatherproof setup.

4

Net Metering Integration

We coordinate with your local electricity board (DISCOM) to install a bi-directional Net Meter. This allows you to export excess power back to the grid and earn credits!

5

Go Live & Start Saving!

Your system is powered on! You get instant access to a smart mobile app to monitor your daily energy generation, while watching your electricity bills drop to zero.
